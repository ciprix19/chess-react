
CHESS RULES

1. pins !
    - bishops, rooks, queens can pin
    - the pinned piece cannot move
2. en-passant -> implement moveHistory, this would be also good for rewatching a match
    - for ranks 4 / 5, pawns can capture enemy pawns that try to move 2 squares
    - enemy pawn on rank 7 pushes to rank 5
    - pawn that is on the 5th rank can take the pawn by going from 5 to 6
3. checks !
    - the king is in check when an enemy piece can see it
    - enemy must escape the check
    - legal moves for escaping check:
        - blocking with another piece
        - moving the king
        - capture the enemy piece
            * WARNING THERE CAN BE MORE THAN ONE PIECE THAT ATTACKS THE KING (DISCOVERED ATTACKS) *
            * IN THIS CASE, CAPTURING BOTH PIECES AT THE SAME TIME IS INHERENTLY NOT POSSIBLEPOSSIBLE *
        - cannot castle!!!
4. castling !
    - king and rook did not move at all
    - there should be not pieces between the king and the rook
    - the squares that the king travel can be attacked (be on check)
    - king cannot be in check and castle
5. checkmate !
    - no legal moves :D
    - cannot block with any other piece
    - cannot move from check (no available square to move to)
    - cannot capture the enemy piece
        * WARNING THERE CAN BE MORE THAN ONE PIECE THAT ATTACKS THE KING (DISCOVERED ATTACKS) *
        * IN THIS CASE, CAPTURING BOTH PIECES AT THE SAME TIME IS INHERENTLY NOT POSSIBLE *
6. promotion done half - can promote every piece not only queen
    - a pawn advanced on the eight rank is promoted to any other piece (except king and pawn)
7. discovered attacks !
    - a piece moves which causes a check on the enemy king but not with the piece that was moved
